Privacy Policy for YYR Tab

YYR Tab is committed to protecting user privacy.

Data Collection: This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) or user data to any remote servers.

Local Storage: All preferences, shortcuts, and configurations are stored locally on the user's device using Chrome's local storage API.

Permissions: Permissions like bookmarks and tabs are used exclusively to provide core functionality (displaying bookmarks and managing open tabs) on the New Tab page.

Third-party Sharing: No data is shared with or sold to third parties.
